Everwinter City

As you turn a corner on the Internet, a whirl of snowflakes greets you. It’s a cold November night; lampposts cast the streets and old buildings in a warm, golden glow. People and cars pass around you; occasionally, you hear jazz and clattering plates from restaurants and cafes, and cars’ honks echo through the city. Some kids are having a snowball fight.
